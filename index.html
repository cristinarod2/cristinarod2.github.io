About Me

I am a Research Program Manager specialized in preclinical imaging, with a clear focus on PET, SPECT, and CT. I design and manage complex imaging workflows, oversee multimodal in vivo and ex vivo studies, and collaborate across disciplines to support translational research. I also enjoy building custom software tools, automated pipelines, and data-driven solutions to advance molecular imaging and streamline lab operations.
